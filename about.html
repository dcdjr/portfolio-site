About Me

I’m a computer science student at URI, pursuing the 4+1 Computer Science to Cybersecurity path as a Schilling Scholar and member of the Honors College. My main interests are in cybersecurity and finance, and I’m always looking for ways to take what I learn in class or on my own and apply it to real-world projects.

Some of my favorite experiences so far include going through Harvard's CS50 and working with the RI Air National Guard on research into VR training. I enjoy learning through hands-on experience, whether it’s experimenting with code or trying out side projects on GitHub.

What drives me most is solving problems and learning along the way. Long-term, I want to work where security and software meet, helping build systems that are both innovative and resilient. In the meantime, I’m open to internships, collaborations, and any opportunity to keep growing while contributing value.
